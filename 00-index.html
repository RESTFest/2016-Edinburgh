About REST Fest

Our objective is to give people interested in REST, Hypermedia APIs, crafting web service APIs, or any related topics a chance to get together in an informal setting to share ideas, trade stories, and show examples of current work.

Keynote: Mike Kelly

Mike Kelly is the founder of Stateless API Consulting. Mike is the creator of the popular Hypertext Application Language (HAL) used by Amazon AppStream, FoxyCart, and many others. Mike and his work have been instrumental in the growth of Hypermedia APIs and the RESTful Web Services API community.
